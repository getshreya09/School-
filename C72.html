Campus Life

School Church

Was established around 1939-1940

On 20 Morrisson Dr, St. Thomas, Ontario is the wide and open community of the St. Anne's Parish. It is the St. Anne's School church and is a very popular Catholic church in St. Thomas.



School Library

Was established around 1939-1940

This library in St. Annes's Elementry School is one of the biggest libraries i have ever seen and it continues to grow each month. It has a wide range of genres in both french and english and has books for all reading levels and our librarian, Mrs. Barlow is one of the best librarians I have ever met



School Gymnasium

Was established around 1939-1940

This gymnasium in St. Annes's Elementry School is one of the biggest gymnasium i have ever seen and it hold so many memories of all the sports games we played against other schools. It is where we play basketball, volleyball, badminton and dance.
